How It Works

Three steps to a complete chronology

1

Upload your records

Drag and drop one or more medical record PDFs — ER reports, treatment notes, imaging results, specialist letters. Any text-based PDF works.

2

AI extracts the timeline

Our system reads every page, identifies medically significant events, and classifies them by type, date, provider, and litigation significance.

3

Download & use

Receive a colour-coded Excel or Word chronology with event dates, types, providers, descriptions, and page references — ready to attach to your case file.
